Programme Officers

Officers & Volunteers

Our Programme Officers handle the day-to-day operations of KBI HK across seven specialised functions. Each officer role is filled by a volunteer community member, typically a current university student or recent graduate.

Role Type Responsibilities
Membership Officer Officer Manages member onboarding, records, and communications. First point of contact for new members and returning participants.
Outreach Officer Officer Manages school and university outreach, ambassador relationships, and community partnerships. Drives participant recruitment for all programmes.
Sponsorship Officer Officer Coordinates sponsor and partner relationships, manages sponsorship packages, and supports the Chapter Director in institutional negotiations.
Events Officer Officer Plans and executes all KBI HK events — workshops, clinics, community gatherings, and competition days. Manages logistics, venues, and volunteer coordination.
Communications Officer Officer Manages KBI HK's public communications across all platforms — social media, newsletters, press releases, and the KBI HK Insight publication.
Associate Programme Officer (APO) APO Junior volunteer role for community members interested in moving into officer positions. APOs support an officer function and learn KBI HK operations through mentored participation.

The APO Programme —
Your Path to the Committee

The Associate Programme Officer (APO) programme is KBI HK's structured pathway for community members who want to move from participant to contributor. APOs are placed with an officer function and take on real responsibilities with mentored guidance.

The APO programme runs for one academic year. Successful APOs are eligible to apply for full Programme Officer roles in the following cycle. Historically, the majority of our current committee came through the APO programme.

1
Apply during open recruitment

APO intake happens twice per year. Applications require a short statement of interest and a description of how you have engaged with KBI HK previously.

2
Matched to an officer function

Based on your interests and availability, you are matched to a Programme Officer who becomes your primary mentor. Functions include events, outreach, communications, and membership.

3
Eligible for full officer role

After a full APO cycle, you may apply for a full Programme Officer role in the next committee intake. References from your mentor are considered in the application.

Learn About Membership First Enquire About APO
Who Should Apply

Current KBI HK members who have participated in at least one programme, competition, or community event

University students or recent graduates based in Hong Kong, available for regular volunteer commitment

Individuals who want to contribute to the operational side of a serious, non-profit, student-run organisation — not just attend events

Those with specific interests in events management, outreach, communications, or community-building in the technology education space

Note: The APO programme is not a prerequisite for Track Director roles. Track Directors are recruited separately based on deep expertise in a specific technology domain.
