Who We Are

A Regional Chapter
with Global Connections

KBI Hong Kong is the Hong Kong regional chapter of the Königsberger Bridges Institute, responsible for localising KBI's mission by building programmes, partnerships, and communities around emerging-technology education and talent development.

We serve as a trusted local gateway for students, schools, universities, employers, mentors, and partners who want serious opportunities in emerging technologies — without the hype, superficiality, or "innovation theatre" that often surrounds the field.

KBI HK is the longest-running KBI chapter globally, with nine years of continuous engagement across Hong Kong's secondary and tertiary education landscape.

Why It Matters

Hong Kong needs
this ecosystem now.

Emerging technologies — blockchain, AI, data science, quantum computing — are reshaping every industry. Hong Kong's position as a global financial, trade, and innovation hub depends on developing a serious pipeline of technically literate, analytically rigorous, and globally competitive young talent.

Without deliberate, sustained investment in education infrastructure that matches the rigour of these technologies, Hong Kong risks losing a generation of talent to better-resourced ecosystems elsewhere.

01

A credible, non-hype platform

KBI HK provides structured, serious programming free from speculative or commercially driven framing — the rigour that serious students and institutions need.

02

Global pathways, locally grounded

As part of the KBI global network, our students and community members connect with international competition stages, research collaborations, and professional pathways.

03

Bridging education and industry

We create the connective tissue between universities, secondary schools, employers, and the technology community — so talent development aligns with real-world needs.

"We are building the ecosystem that Hong Kong's next generation of technologists, researchers, and innovators will grow from — with rigour, integrity, and long-term commitment."

Our History

Nine Years of
Building in Hong Kong

KBI Hong Kong was founded in 2016 as one of the first regional chapters of the Königsberger Bridges Institute. Over nine years, we have built the programmes, partnerships, and community infrastructure that make serious emerging technology education possible for Hong Kong's youth.

Each milestone reflects a sustained commitment to quality over growth — we build what works, deliver it well, and ensure it produces real outcomes for our participants and partners.
