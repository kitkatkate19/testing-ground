Why Join

More Than an Event.
A Sustained Community.

KBI HK membership is for students and early-career talent who want serious, sustained engagement with emerging technologies — not just one-off events. Members gain access to programmes, events, peer networks, mentorship, and leadership opportunities.

Membership is community-first and open to those who bring genuine curiosity, intellectual commitment, and a desire to contribute rather than simply consume.

Membership Types

Find the Right Path for You

KBI HK offers three membership types to serve different audiences. Each path provides access to the community's core benefits with specific orientations toward competition, education, or professional contribution.

Student Member

For secondary and university students in Hong Kong who want access to competitions, training, community, and leadership opportunities in emerging technologies.

  • Competition registration priority
  • Study group and peer network access
  • Mentorship and career guidance
  • Leadership roles in chapter operations
Apply

Educator Member

For teachers and academics who want to connect their students with KBI HK programmes, and who wish to contribute expertise to curriculum, training design, and judging.

  • Institutional referral pathways for students
  • Curriculum alignment support
  • Judging and training design roles
  • Access to educator-specific resources
Apply

Professional Member

For practitioners, alumni, and early-career professionals who want to stay engaged — mentoring, contributing to working groups, and supporting the community.

  • Mentorship and advisory roles
  • Research and working group contribution
  • Competition judging opportunities
  • Speaking and thought leadership platforms
