IDSOL Hong Kong 2023 — Data Science Olympiad Round

The IDSOL HK 2023 qualifying round challenged 22 student teams with real-world datasets across health, urban mobility, and financial risk domains. Teams had 72 hours to produce end-to-end data pipelines and present findings to a panel including HKU statisticians and industry data scientists. Three teams qualified for the IDSOL international finals.

Workshop · Blockchain August 2023 · HKUST, Clear Water Bay
Smart Contract Developer Workshop

An intensive two-day developer workshop covering Solidity programming, smart contract security patterns, and deployment on Ethereum test networks. Led by practitioners from the industry, the workshop included hands-on exercises and a mini-hackathon. 35 students attended, with 12 deploying working contracts by day two.

Alumni June 2023 · Hong Kong
KBI HK Alumni Reunion & Networking Night

The annual alumni gathering brought together past participants across all seven years of KBI HK — a rare opportunity to reconnect across cohorts. Highlights included the "where are they now" spotlight series, informal mentorship tables, and the announcement of the 2023 Scholarship Programme recipients.
