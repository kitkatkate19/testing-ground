Flagship April 2026 · Hong Kong
ETO HK 2026 — Interest Registration Opens

KBI Hong Kong opens registration for the Emerging Technologies Olympiad 2026, spanning all three tracks — blockchain, data science, and quantum computing. The registration launch marked the beginning of the biggest KBI HK programme yet, with record interest from secondary and tertiary institutions.

Community February 2026 · Hong Kong
KBI HK Nine-Year Community Gathering

Marking nine years of continuous operation — the largest KBI HK community gathering to date, bringing together current members, alumni, mentors, and partners. Featured the nine-year retrospective presentation, alumni speakers, and the 2025 scholarship awards.

Competition · Flagship December 2025 · HKUST, Clear Water Bay
ETO HK 2025 — Finals Day

The second edition of ETO HK Finals Day — cross-track challenge presentations from 38 qualifying teams, judged by industry and academic panels. The event drew 280+ attendees including sponsors, institutional partners, and the broader KBI HK community.

Workshop October 2025 · CUHK, Sha Tin
Quantum Primer Workshop Series 2025

Three-session deep-dive into quantum circuit design and Qiskit fundamentals for IQCOL HK 2025 preparation. 45 students attended across four Hong Kong universities. Sessions covered quantum gates, variational quantum eigensolvers, and Qiskit noise models.
