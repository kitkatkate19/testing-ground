Our History

Nine years of events, competitions, workshops, and community — explore our complete visual archive year by year.

The Archive

From Two People with an Idea
to a 1,000-Strong Community

KBI Hong Kong did not begin with a grant or an institutional mandate. It began with a small group of university students who believed Hong Kong's students deserved access to serious emerging-technology education. Nine years later, every event in this archive is proof of what that belief can build. Click a year below to explore.

Founding Philosophy

Built on a Simple Premise

"Hong Kong's students are capable of operating at the international frontier of emerging technology — they just need programmes serious enough to take them there."

This was the founding premise in 2016, and it remains the organising principle of everything KBI Hong Kong builds. Nine years of sustained operation have validated that premise — but the work is far from finished.

KBI Hong Kong operates as a student-led academic chapter of the Königsberger Bridges Institute. We are committed to rigour, curiosity, integrity, and inclusion in emerging-technology education. We do not endorse speculative or financially motivated use of the technologies we teach.
