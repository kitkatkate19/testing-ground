2018 Milestone

KBI Hong Kong launched its inaugural IDSOL HK qualifying round in 2018, becoming one of the first chapters globally to operate both the IBCOL and IDSOL competition tracks concurrently. Member numbers grew to over 120 registered students across six Hong Kong universities.

01
Competition Inaugural Edition November 2018 Hong Kong Polytechnic University

IDSOL HK 2018 — Inaugural Data Science Olympiad

The first-ever Hong Kong qualifying round for the International Data Science Olympiad, held at PolyU's campus in Hung Hom. Ten teams competed in a problem-solving format combining a written examination with a structured data interpretation exercise — assessing statistical reasoning, machine learning fundamentals, and the ability to communicate findings to a non-technical audience. The debut edition exceeded expectations and established IDSOL as a permanent fixture in the KBI HK calendar.

IBCOL HK 2018 — National Qualifying Round

The third edition of the IBCOL Hong Kong qualifying round, held at HKUST for the first time. Fourteen teams competed across four problem domains — an increase from the previous year — reflecting the maturation of blockchain education in Hong Kong's universities. The 2018 cohort included, for the first time, a team from a community college, broadening the chapter's educational reach beyond the research-intensive universities.

03
Symposium June 2018 HKU Centennial Campus

Annual Blockchain & Data Science Symposium 2018

The second annual symposium brought together practitioners, researchers, and students for a day of presentations, panels, and networking. The 2018 edition introduced a poster session for the first time — allowing student researchers to present early-stage work to an informed audience. Industry speakers covered tokenomics, enterprise blockchain adoption, and the growing intersection of machine learning with distributed ledger technology.
