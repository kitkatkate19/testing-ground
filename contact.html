Our Presence

KBI Hong Kong
Operates Across HK

KBI Hong Kong is a non-profit, youth-run chapter that operates primarily across Hong Kong Island, Kowloon, and the New Territories — with events and programmes hosted at partner universities, schools, and venues across the city.

We do not maintain a fixed public office. All enquiries should be directed to our email or via the form above. Programme and event venues are published on the Events page.

Quick Answers

Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I join KBI Hong Kong as a member?

Complete the membership application form on the Membership page. We review all applications personally and aim to respond within 5 business days.

How do students register for competitions?

Competition registration is via the Events page when registration opens, or through the relevant Programme page. Interest registration for ETO HK 2026 opens June 2026.

How can my school partner with KBI HK?

Use the contact form above and select "Institutional Partnership" — or email us directly at hk@kbi.foundation. We respond to institutional partnership enquiries promptly.

Is KBI HK a registered non-profit?

Yes. KBI Hong Kong is a registered non-profit organisation in Hong Kong, operating as a regional chapter of the Königsberger Bridges Institute. We are youth-run and governed by a volunteer team.

How do I become a mentor or advisor?

Use the contact form and select "Mentorship / Advisory". Alternatively, email hk@kbi.foundation with a brief introduction and your area of expertise.

How does sponsorship work?

We offer Bronze, Silver, and Gold sponsorship tiers, plus custom partnership packages. See the Sponsorship Tiers page for details, then use the enquiry form to start the conversation.
