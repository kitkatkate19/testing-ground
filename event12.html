IDSOL HK 2023
Data Science Olympiad Round

KBI HK's first standalone data science competition — a full-day olympiad format with 24 teams, three judging panels, and a dataset challenge that no team had seen before they walked in the door.

14 October 2023
CUHK Business School
24 teams · 150 attendees
Event Recap

The First Standalone IDSOL

IDSOL HK had existed as part of KBI HK's broader programme since 2021, but 2023 was the first year it ran as a fully standalone olympiad — with its own competition format, dedicated judging panel, and a full-day event structure at CUHK's Business School.

The olympiad format was designed to stress-test both technical skills and analytical communication. Teams received an anonymised dataset at 9 AM — Hong Kong public transport passenger flow records from 2018 to 2023 — and had four hours to produce an analytical presentation. No pre-prepared slides were permitted. Judges evaluated both the rigour of the analysis and the clarity of the narrative.

Twenty-four teams competed, with presentations staggered across three parallel judging panels throughout the afternoon. Final scores combined analytical rigour (50%), insight quality (30%), and presentation clarity (20%).

2023 IDSOL Champions

🥇
Team Strata — First Place
HKUST · Division of Social Science
"COVID-19 Recovery Patterns in Hong Kong MTR Ridership: A Segmented Regression Analysis" — Judges praised the methodological rigour, the choice of change-point detection as the analytical frame, and the clarity of the visualisations produced in just four hours.
🥈
Team Echelon — Second Place
HKU · Faculty of Business and Economics
"Peak Demand Forecasting for Transit Network Optimisation: A Multi-Model Approach" — noted for the ambition of their methodology and the practical relevance of the policy recommendations, though the judges felt the model selection rationale needed stronger justification.
🥉
Team Orbit — Third Place
PolyU · Department of Applied Statistics
"Anomaly Detection in Passenger Flow: Identifying Non-Calendar Demand Shocks" — recognised for the creativity of the problem framing and a genuinely useful anomaly detection pipeline built from scratch in four hours.
Legacy

What IDSOL 2023 Established

The 2023 olympiad format — unseen dataset, four-hour analysis window, no pre-prepared materials — became the template for all subsequent IDSOL events. It is also the format that most directly mirrors the style of challenge in industry data science interviews, making it the most career-relevant format KBI HK runs.
