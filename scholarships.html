Our Approach

We Do Things Differently

Our scholarships are not marketing. They are not named trophies. They are a practical commitment to the students who make this organisation worth running.

🎯

No Administrative Deduction

100% of every scholarship donation reaches the recipient. We absorb administrative costs separately — your contribution is not diluted.

🔒

Full Confidentiality

Every application is reviewed in full confidence. Recipients are never publicly identified without their explicit consent. Financial need disclosures are seen only by the committee.

📊

Donor Impact Reporting

Donors receive an annual report detailing who their contribution supported, which programmes they participated in, and what they went on to do. Real accountability — not a thank-you letter.

🤝

Merit and Need Together

We award on merit and on need — sometimes both. Our three award categories ensure that exceptional performers and financially constrained participants both have a path to support.

For Students

Three Ways
We Support You

All applications are reviewed confidentially. There is no competitive shortlist process — each scholarship is evaluated on its own terms.

Need-Based · Annual
KBI HK Access Scholarship
HK$5,000
per recipient
usable across any KBI HK programme
Applications Open

The Access Scholarship removes the financial barrier to participation in KBI HK's paid programmes — ISFRETIC certification training, competition entry fees, and event attendance. It is awarded on demonstrated financial need combined with active membership.

Recipients may apply the award to any KBI HK programme cost within the award year. We do not prescribe how it is used. Unused funds are not carried over to the following year.

Who can apply
Active KBI HK member for at least 3 months
Current secondary school or university student in Hong Kong
Demonstrated financial need (supporting documentation required)
Participation in at least one KBI HK programme or event
Need-Based Annual Apply via Contact Form
Merit-Based · Per Cycle
KBI HK Excellence Award
HK$8,000
per recipient · 2 awards per cycle
(secondary + university)
Auto-Considered

The Excellence Award recognises outstanding competition performance — not just technical results, but the quality of analysis, rigour of research, and depth of collaboration a team demonstrates throughout the process.

Two awards are made per cycle: one for secondary school participants, one for university participants. No separate application is required. Every competition finalist is automatically considered by the judging committee. Recipients are publicly recognised at the annual KBI HK gathering.

Who is considered
Participant in IBCOL HK, IDSOL HK, IQCOL HK, or ETO HK
Active KBI HK member at time of competition
Selection made by judging committee — no application required
Merit-Based Competition Award No Application Needed
Project-Based · Rolling
KBI HK Research Bursary
HK$3,000
per project member
up to HK$12,000 per team of 4
Rolling Applications

The Research Bursary supports KBI HK members pursuing approved research or technology development projects under the KBI HK Tech Projects programme. Funding covers direct project costs: software tools, computing resources, conference fees, and publication costs.

Teams of up to 4 members may apply jointly. Applications require a project proposal reviewed and approved by the KBI HK Projects committee. Rolling applications are accepted year-round while funds are available.

Who can apply
Active KBI HK member, enrolled in a university programme
Registered project under the KBI HK Tech Projects programme
Project proposal reviewed and approved by committee
Teams of 1–4 members may apply together

The Process

How to Apply

Applying for the Access Scholarship or Research Bursary takes less than 30 minutes. The Excellence Award requires no application at all.

1

Check Eligibility

Review the criteria for the scholarship you want to apply for. If you are unsure, reach out — we would rather answer a question than lose a qualified applicant.

2

Prepare Your Documents

For the Access Scholarship: a short personal statement and supporting documentation of financial need. For the Research Bursary: a project proposal (template provided on request).

3

Submit via Contact Form

Send your application through our contact form, marking it "Scholarship Application." Include your membership status, the scholarship you are applying for, and your attached documents.

4

Committee Review

The Scholarship Committee reviews all applications within 3 weeks. You will be notified directly via email, regardless of outcome. All decisions are made confidentially.

Apply Now via Contact Form

Questions? Email us at hk@kbi.org — we reply within 48 hours.
