ETO Hong Kong 2026 — Registration Opens This Summer

KBI Hong Kong announces the launch of interest registration for the Emerging Technologies Olympiad Hong Kong 2026 — the chapter's flagship competition spanning all three emerging-technology tracks: blockchain (IBCOL), data science (IDSOL), and quantum computing (IQCOL).

ETO HK 2026 represents the first time KBI Hong Kong has brought together all three competition tracks under a single unified competitive stage, with a structured progression pathway to KBI's international competitions.

About KBI HK

KBI Hong Kong is the regional chapter of the Königsberger Bridges Institute, established in 2016. It is a non-profit, youth-run organisation focused on emerging-technology education.

Key Facts
  • ·Established 2016 — longest-running KBI chapter globally
  • ·1,000+ community members across HK
  • ·3 core technology tracks: Blockchain, Data Science, Quantum
  • ·50+ programmes and events delivered
