About KBI Hong Kong

Who We Are

KBI Hong Kong is part of the Königsberger Bridges Institute (KBI), an international nonprofit named after the Königsberg Bridges Problem — the mathematical puzzle that Euler solved in 1736 by inventing an entirely new field. We've been doing the same with students ever since.

We bring together students, educators, researchers, and industry leaders to do what most institutions don't: connect rigorous thinking with real-world application, from day one.
